Wakanda Chamber of Commerce – Site Plan

Site Purpose

The Wakanda Chamber of Commerce website serves as a hub for innovation, investment, and collaboration in Wakanda. It connects investors and entrepreneurs, promotes local business opportunities, and shares event updates that foster economic growth and community engagement.

Target Audience

  • Primary: Investors and Entrepreneurs seeking business opportunities in Wakanda.
  • Secondary: Local business owners, organizations, and community leaders.

Site Goals

  • Attract new members to the Chamber of Commerce.
  • Promote local business opportunities and partnerships.
  • Share upcoming events and initiatives that support innovation.

User Personas

  • T’Lani – Investor: 42-year-old venture capitalist seeking sustainable projects and tech innovation in Wakanda.
  • Nia – Entrepreneur: 32-year-old tech founder seeking networking opportunities and mentorship.
  • Jabari – Community Leader: 50-year-old civic leader connecting local businesses with government initiatives.

Site Structure

The website will include the following responsive pages:

  • Home: Attention-grabbing hero banner, community highlights, weather, local business spotlights, and CTAs.
  • Discover: Local history, demographics, image montage, and current events.
  • Directory: List of local businesses and organizations (sortable/filterable).
  • Join: Information on membership benefits, levels (Non-Profit, Silver, Gold), and an application form.

SEO & Accessibility Plan

  • Use relevant keywords: “Wakanda business,” “Chamber of Commerce,” “invest in Wakanda.”
  • Include meta descriptions, alt text for images, and proper heading hierarchy.
  • Embed Google Analytics to track engagement.
  • Ensure all color contrasts and text sizes meet WCAG standards.

Design & Branding

  • Primary Color: Deep Purple (#4B0082)
  • Secondary Color: Gold (#D4AF37)
  • Background: White or Charcoal (#FFFFFF / #1C1C1C)
  • Text Color: Warm White or Deep Charcoal (#F5F5F5 / #222222)
  • Typography: Playfair Display (Headings), Lora (Body)
  • Overall Mood: Innovative, elegant, and inspiring — blending tradition and technology.

Technical Specifications

  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript maintained in separate files.
  • Fully responsive using @media queries for mobile, tablet, and desktop views.
  • Consistent navigation bar and footer across all pages.
  • Clean, semantic markup (using header, nav, main, section, footer elements).
  • Optimized images for faster load times.
